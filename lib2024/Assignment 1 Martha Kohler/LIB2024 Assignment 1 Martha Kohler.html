My favorite movie: Dirty Dancing (1987)

Dirty Dancing tells the story of Frances "Baby" Houseman, a young woman who falls in love with dance instructor Johnny Castle at a family resort in upstate New York in the 1960s. When Johnny's dance partner falls ill and is unable to dance with him in an upcoming competition, Baby offers to stand in and learn the moves. The film also covers serious themes of abortion, class conflict, and complex family dynamics which are still relevant today, despite the film's 1987 release.

Cast List:

  • Jennifer Grey as Frances "Baby" Houseman
  • Patrick Swayze as Johnny Castle
  • Cynthia Rhodes as Penny Johnson
  • Jerry Orbach as Jake Houseman
  • Jane Brucker as Lisa Houseman
  • Kelly Bishop as Marjorie Houseman
